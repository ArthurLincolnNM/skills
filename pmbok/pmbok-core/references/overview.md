# PMBOK 6th — Overview

## Project Management Framework

### What is a Project?
A **temporary endeavor** undertaken to create a **unique product, service, or result**. Temporary = has a beginning and end. Unique = produces something distinct.

### What is Project Management?
Applying **knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques** to project activities to meet requirements.

### The 5 Process Groups

| PG | Purpose | # Processes | Key Output |
|----|---------|------------|-----------|
| **Initiating** | Define a new project or phase. Authorize it. | 2 | Project Charter, Stakeholder Register |
| **Planning** | Establish scope, refine objectives, define action. | 24 | Project Management Plan |
| **Executing** | Complete the work, deliver deliverables. | 10 | Deliverables, work performance data |
| **Monitoring & Controlling** | Track, review, adjust. Keep on track. | 12 | Change requests, performance info |
| **Closing** | Formally finalize. Archive. Lessons learned. | 1 | Final product, archived records |

### Tailoring
Tailoring = adjusting project management approaches based on the **nature of the project**, organizational environment, stakeholder needs.

### Agile / Hybrid
PMBOK 6 embraces adaptive approaches. Hybrid = combining predictive (waterfall) and agile.

### Project Benefits
Project Benefits Management Plan = document what benefits will be delivered and when.
